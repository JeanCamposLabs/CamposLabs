# EvenFee

**Recover the Amazon FBA fees you were overcharged.**

EvenFee helps Amazon FBA sellers identify and recover fulfillment and storage fees they were incorrectly charged due to inaccurate product dimension and weight measurements in their inventory records.

## The Problem

Amazon calculates FBA fees based on product dimensions and weight. A single measurement error compounds across thousands of units—quietly adding up to significant overcharges month after month. Most sellers never discover these errors until it's too late.

## The Solution

EvenFee audits your FBA inventory for dimension and weight discrepancies, quantifies the overcharges, and guides you through the recovery process with Amazon.

- **Accurate Audits**: Verify actual product dimensions against recorded measurements
- **Detailed Reports**: Clear documentation of discrepancies and corresponding fee overcharges
- **Recovery Support**: Step-by-step guidance for Amazon reimbursement claims

## About

EvenFee is built for Amazon FBA sellers who take their business seriously. The service adheres to the highest standards of data security and privacy, with zero access to customer or buyer information—only the operational data needed to conduct the audit.
